Focaccia Recipe

Description

This recipe is for making a fluffy focaccia bread. You can top the focaccia simply with salt and oil, or add decoration

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Place the flour in a food mix
  2. Add a sachet of yeast to a jar containing 500ml of warm water, a spoon of sugar and a pinch of salt
  3. Pour the water and yeast mix into the mixer and mix forr 5 mins
  4. Allow to rise for 1 hour
  5. Cook for 45 min in a wood fired oven for best results
